the world runs on a real clock

The creatures follow the real sun.

Every creature has two faces — a daylit one, and something worse after dark. Which you meet isn't a menu setting; it's decided by the sun over your actual street, the same clock that colors the whole game.

one loop, endlessly deep

Walk. Fight. Loot. Repeat.

Walk to summon

Real GPS distance accrues as you move — even in your pocket, even in the background. Cover ground and the encounters come to you. No spawns to farm; a walk is the dungeon.

Fight in turns

Turn-based duels with room to think. Three archetypes — Warrior, Rogue, Mage — each with its own resource and a kit of abilities: strikes, bleeds, wards, and burns. Elites hit back.

Loot that matters

Affix-based, procedurally generated gear across every slot, from common to legendary — Diablo-grade itemization on a walker. Equip it, re-spec around it, chase the god roll.
