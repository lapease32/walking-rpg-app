Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

StrideQuest is a location-based role-playing game. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Data We Collect

Location

The app uses your device's GPS to measure walking distance and generate in-game encounters. Location data is processed on-device in real time and is never stored on our servers. If you have granted background location permission, tracking continues when the app is minimised so that walk sessions are not interrupted.

Player Progress

Your in-game data — level, experience, inventory, equipment, total distance walked, and encounter history — is saved to Google Firebase Firestore so it can be restored if you reinstall or switch devices. This data is stored under a unique identifier (anonymous or linked to your sign-in account) and is not shared with third parties.

Account Information

The app creates an anonymous account automatically when you first launch it. If you choose to sign in with Google or Apple, your display name and email address are used solely to identify your account and are not used for marketing or shared with third parties.

Crash Reports

The app uses Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports. These reports include device model, operating system version, and app state at the time of the crash. No personally identifiable information is included.

Analytics

The app uses Firebase Analytics to collect anonymised gameplay events such as encounters triggered, combat outcomes, and session length. This data helps us understand how the game is played and improve the experience. It is not linked to your identity and is not sold.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

  • Location history is not stored on our servers at any point.
  • We do not collect payment information. Any in-app purchases are handled entirely by Apple or Google.
  • We do not serve advertisements and do not use advertising identifiers.
  • We do not sell your data to any third party.

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Crashlytics, Analytics) — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Sign in with Google — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Sign in with Apple — apple.com/legal/privacy

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Your player data is retained for as long as you use the app. To delete your data, contact us at lapease32@gmail.com and we will remove your Firestore record within 30 days. You may also sign out and uninstall the app, which removes all locally stored data from your device.

5. Children's Privacy

StrideQuest is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last revised. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact

Questions or requests regarding your data: lapease32@gmail.com
